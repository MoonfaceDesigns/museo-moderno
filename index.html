A Modern Classic

Suited for use by all those who feel an affinity for the modern and contemporary, designed through analyzing the history and essence of the Beunos Aires Museum of Modern Art in Argentina.

A Design Journey

Designed by Pablo Casgoya and Omnibus Type, Museo Moderno is the result of a two-year quest that witnessed initial completion in 2019, with the typeface fully completed in 2022.
